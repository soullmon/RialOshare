How To Use RialOshare

  1. Select the material to display on the banner.
  2. Enter your nickname X for the profile.
  3. Upload an avatar/profile image from your device.
  4. Choose a role (optional) to show on the banner.
  5. Click 'Generate Banner' to create the banner with a random background and decoration.
  6. Download the banner using 'Download'.
  7. Share it to X (Twitter) using 'Share to X'.
