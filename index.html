How To Use RialOshare Generator

  1. Select the material to display on the banner.
  2. Enter your nickname X for the profile.
  3. Upload an avatar/profile image from your device.
  4. Choose a role (optional) to show on the banner.
  5. Click 'Generate Banner' to create the banner with a random background and top-layer decoration.
  6. Download the created banner using the 'Download' button.
  7. Share the banner to X (Twitter) using the 'Share to X' button.
